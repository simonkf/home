Crystal Mimicry

What My Power Is

Crystal Mimicry is the ability to transform my body completely into crystal or crystalline substance. My transformed form is anatomically identical to my normal form. I am immune to any form of toxins, venom, viruses, bacteria, allergens and diseases. I can protect myself by creating dermal body armour; which is resistant to attacks, harm or pain and temperature extremes. I have the power to utilize weapons that I can create naturally with my powers. I can neutralize my sensitivity to physical pain; which comes in handy in my line of work. I have a high physical durability, allowing me to take numerous blows of internal or external assaults before succumbing to the effects and I have enhanced strength. My lungs are powerful allowing me to breathe underwater. I can resist high intensities of light and heat.

How I Obtained My Powers

My powers are passed down as a mantle to worthy family members in every generation and I've been training to be the next worthy family member of my generation. I had to prove myself worthy in all forms of combat and mental training. Finally, I was given the gemstone that allowed me to use my powers at a full maximum.

My Job

I work as an agent for an extra-governmental military counter-terrorism and intelligence agency, tasked with maintaining global security. I'm tasked with helping counter terrorism and shut down illegal organizations; like Hydra. I also go on secret missions around the world. I'm only allowed to use my powers for the good of mankind and not for my own personal gain.
