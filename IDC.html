The Meaning of IDC

The “I” stands for interesting, the “D” stands for different and the “C” stands for clever.

Interesting

Different

The difference in your CSS is that you did not do anything extravagant. Your CSS was very basic and different from most people's, you stuck to being simple.

Clever

It's clever that you made sure all your colours complimented each other and none of them were too bright that they were blinding or too dark that it was hard to see. You kept your web page simple which gave it a professional and serious look.
